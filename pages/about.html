about

You'd learn a lot more about me if you scheduled an interview... but here is what you should maybe know before you call.

I am an enthusiast, a gearhead, a tech nerd and a dog dad.

I go from pastime to pastime obsessively researching, planning and purchasing. I love cars, drones, phones, home improvement, AV, movies, skeet-shooting, exercising, soccer, traveling and working.

My design process is much the same. First I ask a lot of questions, to figure out what the problem is and how it is affecting people. I then spend the majority of my time analyizing and researching. It's rare to be working on something truly original – so why start from square 1 when you don't have to?

After I understand the problem and have seen how other people have solved it, I start designing. 4-colored-pen hits graph paper, followed shortly by digital wireframes. Low fidelity is all on paper, high fidelity is on the computer. I build the best design, then measure and rebuild.
